About Mildred J. Hamilton, Esquire

Mildred has been a real estate attorney in New Jersey for over 30 years. She received her law degree from Seton Hall Law School. Mildred works with buyers and sellers throughout the state with everything from contract review, repair requests, title review, survey review, closing processing, and more. She will represent your legal interests from the moment you've found a house until closing. Along with practicing real estate law, Mildred also prepares wills and powers of attorney and does estate planning. Her practice is located in Florence, New Jersey.
